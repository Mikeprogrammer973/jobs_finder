Senior Web Engineer for a High-Volume Shopify Seller.

We are a health and wellness company and have been in business for over 12 years in Wilmington, DE. Join our passionate team as a Senior Engineer and elevate our online stores to the next level. We are an organization built around the mission of helping improve people’s lives. We're searching for a skilled and experienced coder who thrives in a collaborative environment and possesses a deep understanding of ecommerce best practices. This position is full-time, in-person. Generous relocation package offered plus signing bonus.

Responsibilities:

  • Help develop and maintain high-performing Shopify stores and themes using Liquid, HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and React.
  • Build and support custom Shopify apps, extensions, and functions.
  • Aid in the expansion and maintenance of our backend systems – we use NestJS, Postgres, and Python.
  • Engage actively in a closely collaborative team of engineers and developers.
  • Oversee the active performance of our sites and backend systems.
  • Assist in optimizing conversion rates through UX/UI improvements and automated testing.
  • Implement custom functionality and integrations to ensure a seamless customer experience.
  • Collaborate directly with stakeholders, including designers, marketers, and product managers to bring value to our customers.
  • Contribute to the planning, prioritizing, and execution of development projects using agile methodologies.
  • Utilize project management software like Jira to effectively track progress and manage deliverables.
  • Maintain code quality, adhere to coding standards, and best practices.
  • Contribute to our code libraries and custom tools.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of experience with front-end development at a mid-senior level.
  • Extensive experience in Shopify theme development, configuration, and app creation.
  • Strong proficiency in HTML5, SCSS, Vanilla JS, jQuery, and Liquid scripting.
  • Proven ability to build dynamic and responsive user interfaces.
  • Familiarity with command-line tools and development workflows.
  • Experience with JS frameworks and libraries (we use React).
  • Backend engineering experience.
  • Some familiarity with NodeJS and TypeScript.
  • Solid understanding of ecommerce practices and conversion optimization techniques.
  • Experience with project management tools like Jira.
  • Proficiency in version control systems (we use Git).

Bonus Points:

  • Proficiency with NestJS and Postgres.
  • Knowledge of stack observability tooling (Datadog, Prometheus, New Relic).
  • Experience working in any of the following contexts:
  • Creating and maintaining APIs
  • Lerna
  • Advanced CSS techniques and tools including TailwindCSS, OOCSS, SMA, etc.
  • Cloudflare Workers and Pages
  • Python
  • Rust
  • MVC or similar web frameworks such as ExpressJS, Meteor, Django, or Laravel.
  • SQL and relational database building
  • Alternatives to React, like AlpineJS

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package, commensurate with experience.
  • Opportunity to work with a talented and passionate team on cutting-edge projects.
  • Continuous learning and development opportunities.
  • A chance to make a real impact on an ecommerce business focused on improving people’s lives.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $90,000.00 - $150,000.00 per year

Work Location: In person
